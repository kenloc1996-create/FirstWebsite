About the game

Limbus Company is a single-player turn-based role-playing game developed and published by Project Moon. It was released on February 27, 2023 for Microsoft Windows, iOS, and Android. It is the studio's third game, set in the same world as Lobotomy Corporation and Library of Ruina.

The Lore of Limbus Company

The game takes place in a dark urban mega-city simply called "the City," ruled by powerful megacorporations known as Wings. The protagonist, Dante, is an amnesiac whose head was replaced with a prosthetic clock after a violent confrontation with a mysterious faction. Dante is then recruited as the Executive Manager of the Limbus Company Bus Department (LCB).

Together with twelve individuals known as the "Sinners," Dante travels across the City aboard a special vehicle called Mephistopheles — a bus with unique abilities. Their mission: to collect the Golden Boughs, the remnants of energy production technology left behind at old Lobotomy Corporation facilities.

Dante holds the power to reverse time, allowing them to heal the Sinners or even bring them back from death — but at the cost of personally feeling every injury and death they undo. The story is structured like Dante's Inferno, divided into chapters called Cantos (main story) and Intervallos (side stories).

The Twelve sinner

Each Sinner is inspired by a character from classic world literature:

  • Yi Sang — Based on the Korean modernist writer Yi Sang. Possesses mirror technology crucial to the LCB's mission.
  • Faust — Based on the scholar Faust from Goethe's tragedy. Acts as the de facto leader of the Sinners.
  • Don Quixote — Based on the idealistic knight from Cervantes' novel. Cheerful and fiercely loyal.
  • Ryoshu — Based on the Japanese writer Ryunosuke Akutagawa. A skilled and unpredictable fighter.
  • Meursault — Based on the detached protagonist of Camus' The Stranger. Stoic and tremendously capable.
  • Hong Lu — Based on the nobleman Jia Baoyu from Dream of the Red Chamber. Wealthy and easygoing.
  • Heathcliff — Based on the brooding anti-hero of Wuthering Heights. Quick to anger, fights with a metal bat.
  • Ishmael — Based on the narrator of Moby Dick. A pragmatic survivor and voice of reason on the bus.
  • Rodion — Based on Raskolnikov from Crime and Punishment. Energetic and unpredictable.
  • Sinclair — Based on Emil Sinclair from Demian by Hermann Hesse. Young and still finding his place.
  • Outis — Based on Odysseus from Homer's Odyssey. Tactical and composed under pressure.
  • Gregor — Based on Gregor Samsa from Kafka's The Metamorphosis. Carries a heavy burden and quiet sadness.

Gameplay

Limbus Company is a turn-based RPG with gacha elements. Players manage a team of Sinners and equip them with different Identities — alternate forms drawn from various factions and corporations across the City. Identities change a Sinner's appearance, skills, health, speed, and sin resistances.

Each Sinner also has access to powerful abilities called E.G.O, which grow stronger — and more dangerous — at low sanity. Using E.G.O while a Sinner is barely holding on triggers E.G.O Corrosion, a distorted but devastating form of the attack.

Key game modes include:

  • Story chapters (Cantos) — the main narrative chapters following Dante and the Sinners.
  • Mirror Dungeons — roguelike runs for farming resources.
  • Refraction Railway — challenging endgame content for experienced players.
